Pappala Praveen is Director and Founder of our Organisation. He came up with a thought that very farmer should be benefited and able to stand on his own, then he started his organisation and named it as PP Dairy Farm. With support of his family members and some local farmers, He started this farm with a small capacity and now this farm's product is reaching to every corner of our state.

At PP Farms, our humble mission is to serve cows with love and devotion. In the process, we engage in protecting cows, promoting the growth of desi cows and their products, and helping farmers to get back to organic farming with the help of cows. This enables us to serve our customers with traditional dairy and food products leading us to a happier and healthier lifestyle.

PROTECT COWS

When cows stop giving milk, we don't sell to open markets. We maintain them or handover to Goshalas to complete their life.

PROMOTE DESI COWS

Desi cow milk, milk products, cow urine, and dung are an essential part of our life. We promote desi cow products to our customers.

ORGANIC FARMING

Desi cow urine and cow dung play a vital role in organic farming. We can make various types of organic fertilizers for organic farming.

FOLLOW TRADITION

Our elders lead a happy and healthy lifestyle by keeping the cow in the center. We believe organic dairy and organic products are a way to go.

NATURAL PRODUCTS

Our mission to give our customers with what nature gives to us without alteration. We focus on natural dairy and food products.

HEALTHY LIFESTYLE

Consuming natural dairy and food products will help us lead disease-free and happy lifestyle. We want our customers to join this mission.

OUR PROCESS

We follow the farm-to-home concept. We avoid many layers in between. After milking is done, milk is chilled at remote chilling centers, transported to our main plant, packed, and delivered to your home as quickly as possible with a strict cold chain.

Milking

Cows are washed regularly and their hygiene is maintained very well. Before milking udder area is thoroughly washed and dried.

Chilling

After milking, milk is chilled as quickly as possible to 4°C. This helps to avoid bacterial growth and maintain freshness.

Packaging

At 2-4°C, we start packing and store in cold rooms at 2°C. We strictly follow hygenic and covid rules while packaging and loading into trucks.

Home Delivery

Fresh milk is delivered to customers every day before 7 am to their doorsteps. We maintain a strict cold chain end-to-end to maintain freshness.
